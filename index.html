Barbie's Magical Movie Catalog

Welcome to a wondrous collection of Barbie's most enchanting adventures! Explore her timeless tales of courage, friendship, and believing in yourself.

Barbie in the Nutcracker (2001)

Synopsis

Clara receives a beautiful wooden Nutcracker which magically comes to life to protect her from the evil Mouse King. She embarks on a journey to find the Sugarplum Princess and break the spell.

Moral of the Story

Anything is possible if you are brave, clever, and believe in yourself.

Barbie as Rapunzel (2002)

Synopsis

Trapped in a tower by the evil witch Gothel, Rapunzel discovers a magic paintbrush that allows her to paint portals and escape her prison, leading her to find love and her true destiny.

Moral of the Story

Creativity and love can change the world. Honesty will always set you free.

Barbie of Swan Lake (2003)

Synopsis

Odette, a baker's daughter, follows a unicorn into an enchanted forest. There, the evil wizard Rothbart curses her to be a swan by day. She must find her courage to save the forest and break the spell.

Moral of the Story

You are braver than you think. Even an ordinary person can do extraordinary things.

The Princess and the Pauper (2004)

Synopsis

Princess Anneliese and the poor seamstress Erika, two identical girls, switch places to save the kingdom from the villainous Preminger. Their friendship blossoms as they follow their dreams.

Moral of the Story

Live your dream. True freedom comes from following your heart and being true to yourself.

Barbie: Fairytopia (2005)

Synopsis

Elina, a wingless flower fairy, must embark on a journey to find the Guardian Fairy Azura when a sickness begins to kill all the flowers and stop the fairies from flying in her home of Magic Meadow.

Moral of the Story

What makes you different is what makes you special. True wings are earned through courage and friendship.

The Magic of Pegasus (2005)

Synopsis

Princess Annika escapes the clutches of the evil wizard, Wenlock, with the help of a magnificent winged horse, Brietta. Together they must build the Wand of Light to break Wenlock's spells.

Moral of the Story

There is always hope. Family, whether by birth or by choice, is the greatest strength.

The Barbie Diaries (2006)

Synopsis

In this modern tale, high school sophomore Barbie finds confidence through a magical diary that makes her dreams come true, but she soon learns the importance of hard work and being herself.

Moral of the Story

Confidence comes from believing in yourself, not from magic. Good things happen when you work for them.

Fairytopia: Mermaidia (2006)

Synopsis

Elina travels to Mermaidia to save her friend Prince Nalu from the clutches of the evil Laverna. To do so, she must make a great sacrifice: her wings for a mermaid's tail.

Moral of the Story

True friendship requires sacrifice. Trust is the most powerful magic of all.

The 12 Dancing Princesses (2006)

Synopsis

Princess Genevieve and her eleven sisters discover a secret entrance to a magical world where they can dance all night. But when their father is in danger, they must use their unity to save him and the kingdom.

Moral of the Story

Together, there's nothing you can't do. The big and small differences between us make our family strong.

Barbie as the Island Princess (2007)

Synopsis

After being shipwrecked on an island as a child, Princess Rosella grows up with a family of loving animals. When a prince discovers their island paradise, she journeys to his kingdom and uncovers her past.

Moral of the Story

Love can build a bridge to wherever you want to go. Being true to who you are is your greatest gift.

Fairytopia: Magic of the Rainbow (2007)

Synopsis

Elina travels to the Crystal Palace to attend fairy school and learn the annual Flight of Spring. However, the evil Laverna returns with a plan to plunge Fairytopia into a ten-year winter. Elina and her new friends must work together to stop her.

Moral of the Story

Together we are stronger. Unity and collaboration are more powerful than working alone.

Barbie & the Diamond Castle (2008)

Synopsis

Best friends Liana and Alexa embark on a dangerous journey to the hidden Diamond Castle to rescue their new friend, Melody, who is trapped inside a magic mirror, and end the rule of the evil Lydia.

Moral of the Story

Friendship is the true treasure. Music and companionship can overcome any obstacle.

A Christmas Carol (2008)

Synopsis

This film is an adaptation of Charles Dickens's book, "A Christmas Carol and Other Christmas Stories." In this adaptation, Barbie plays Eden Starling, a glamorous but selfish singing diva in Victorian England. She is visited by three Christmas Spirits who teach her the true meaning of the holiday.

Moral of the Story

It's never too late to change. The spirit of Christmas is about kindness, generosity, and keeping love in your heart.

Mariposa & Her Butterfly Fairy Friends (2008)

Synopsis

Mariposa is a butterfly fairy who loves to read and dream about the world outside her home in Flutterfield. When the Queen is poisoned by the evil fairy Henna, Mariposa must bravely venture into dangerous lands to find an antidote and save her kingdom.

Moral of the Story

The best thing you can be is yourself. You don't have to fit in to be a true hero.

Barbie Presents: Thumbelina (2009)

Synopsis

Thumbelina is a tiny Twillerbee who lives in a field of wildflowers. When a spoiled girl digs up her patch, she and her friends must prove that even the smallest person can make a big difference.

Moral of the Story

Even the smallest person can make a huge difference. We must all work together to care for our environment.

The Three Musketeers (2009)

Synopsis

Corinne, a country girl, dreams of becoming a musketeer. She travels to Paris and joins three other girls who share the same dream. Together, they must use their unique skills to protect Prince Louis.

Moral of the Story

True courage and justice are for everyone. Girls can do anything they set their minds to.

Barbie in a Mermaid Tale (2010)

Synopsis

Merliah Summers, a champion surfer from Malibu, discovers a shocking family secret: she's a half-mermaid and princess of Oceana. She must embrace her new identity to save her mother and the underwater kingdom.

Moral of the Story

What makes you different might just be your greatest strength.

A Fashion Fairytale (2010)

Synopsis

Barbie travels to Paris to visit her aunt's fashion house, only to find it's about to be shut down. With the help of three magical Flairies, she uses her creativity to put on a show and save the business.

Moral of the Story

Magic happens when you believe in yourself. Don't be afraid to express your creativity.

Barbie: A Fairy Secret (2011)

Synopsis

When Ken is suddenly whisked away by a group of fairies, Barbie and her rival Raquelle must travel to the secret fairy world of Gloss Angeles to rescue him. They learn that friendship is the real magic.

Moral of the Story

Forgiveness is a powerful tool. The best magic of all is friendship.

Princess Charm School (2011)

Synopsis

Blair Willows wins a lottery to attend the prestigious Princess Charm School. There, she must learn to be a royal lady while uncovering a plot and discovering her true identity as the kingdom's long-lost princess.

Moral of the Story

There's a princess in every girl. Your character and kindness matter more than your background.

The Princess & the Popstar (2012)

Synopsis

Princess Tori longs for the exciting life of a popstar, while popstar Keira dreams of being a princess. They magically switch places and discover that the other life isn't as easy as it looks.

Moral of the Story

The best thing you can be is yourself. Appreciate the life you have.
